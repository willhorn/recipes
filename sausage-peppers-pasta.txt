Ingredients:
 - 3-5 sweet Italian Sausages
 - 1-2 diced bell peppers
 - Half-Full box of pasta
 - Extra virgin olive oil
 - Salt

Directions:
 1. Dice peppers first.
 2. Then cut raw sausage into bite-sized pieces.
 3. Cook pasta according to directions. Add a little olive oil and salt to the water. 
 4. While pasta is cooking, saute sausage in a little olive oil.
 5. When the sausage isn't totally pink anymore, add the peppers and saute until the meat is cooked through.
 6. When the pasta is done, drain and put back in pot.
 7. Pour sausage/pepper mixture into the pot and mix.
